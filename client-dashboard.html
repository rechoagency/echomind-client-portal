How Document Intelligence Works

Your documents are processed using advanced AI to understand and reference your brand content intelligently.

Document Processing
  • Text Extraction: All uploaded documents are read and processed to extract text content
  • Smart Chunking: Content is split into meaningful sections (chunks) for better context understanding
  • Vector Embeddings: Each chunk is converted into a 1536-dimensional mathematical representation (vector) using OpenAI's embedding model
Intelligent Retrieval
  • Semantic Search: When generating responses, EchoMind searches your documents by meaning, not just keywords
  • Context-Aware: Finds the most relevant information based on the conversation context
  • Real-time Reference: Pulls accurate information from your brand guidelines, FAQs, and product docs to craft authentic responses
Why This Matters: Instead of generic AI responses, EchoMind references your actual brand voice, product specifications, and guidelines to create authentic, on-brand content that accurately represents your business. The more comprehensive your documents, the more intelligent and accurate your automated responses become.
